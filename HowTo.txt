| HOW TO USE YOUR OWN IMAGES? |

Drag Your Image Into this Folder then open Config.json with A Text Editor
Then You will See "fullImgName" Put the Name of your file in quotes like this

"catgirl.jpg" 
best if you turn on file name extensions!

| How to adjust Image Speed? |
Go to Config.json then you will see speedX and speedY,
Change both the numbers to your liking :)
